Benchmark · Reference Title

Stardew Valley

The definitive cozy farming-life sim, and our clearest quality bar for loop, tone, and approachability. This page separates what we emulate from what we rebuild.

TL;DR

Stardew nails the "just one more day" loop, gentle onboarding, and NPCs worth caring about — all of which we want. But its Pelican Town setting, festivals, and folklore are American-pastoral; we keep the structure of those systems and refill every one with Meitei content from the Knowledge Bible.

Why it's our primary benchmark

Loop

Daily rhythm we want to match

Cozy

NPCs

Onboarding

Easy to start, deep to master

What we take, adapt, and reject

Emulate

The core day loop; energy/time budget; forgiving pace; friendship hearts; a town that slowly opens up; readable pixel-adjacent clarity.

Adapt

Seasonal festivals → Meitei festivals; crops/tools → paddy, ngari, local implements; the calendar → the Meitei lunar-solar year and Lallup labour duty.

Reject

Western pastoral aesthetic; wizard/Junimo fantasy; generic RPG combat mine; anything that implies the wrong economy or religion for the era.
