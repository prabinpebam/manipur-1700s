Art & Audio Direction

How the valley looks and sounds — a period reconstruction, not today's heritage-site version of Manipur.

TL;DR

Art direction reconstructs the chosen year: timber-and-brick architecture, natural-dye costume, and a soundscape rooted in Meitei music and language. Crucially, monuments like Kangla are depicted as they stood then — e.g. without the Kangla Sha statues, which were added in 1804.

P2

Reconstruct the year, not the postcard

Don't recreate today's Kangla. Depict it as it stood in the chosen year — traditional Meitei style, timber with brick, brightly painted, royal sentries, and no Kangla Sha dragon-lion statues (first erected 1804). This is a rare chance to visualise 18th-century Manipur.
